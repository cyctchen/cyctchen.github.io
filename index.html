1. Signals and Systems: A Fresh Look

One year before my retirement in 2011, I developed Signals and Systems: A Fresh Look as my retirement gift to my students and to anyone else interested in the material. Signals and Systems is now a required course in most EE and ECE programs and more than thirty textbooks, including my own, are available. However, after teaching the course for over two decades and realizing that much of what I taught was not used in practice, I developed, after another decade of pondering and searching, this book at the end of my teaching career.

Signals and Systems: A Fresh Look can be downloaded as a FREE PDF (5MB, 405pp) using the preceding link. A hardcopy can also be ordered from Amazon for $22. A handwritten solutions manual is available.
